Miss a call and an automatic text goes out within seconds — "Sorry we missed you, how can we help?" — so the lead becomes a conversation instead of a call to the next company. Google profile management

Your Google Business Profile is how nearby customers find you and decide whether to call. We keep it sharp — accurate hours and services, fresh photos and posts, and prompt, professional replies to every review — so you rank higher in local search and look like the obvious choice.
