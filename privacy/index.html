Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Sentriq Digital ("Sentriq," "we," "us," or "our") provides digital marketing and growth services to businesses, including website design, search engine optimization, Google Business Profile management, and lead-capture tools. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you give us — such as your name, business name, email address, and phone number when you request a quote or contact us.
  • Client business data — for clients who engage our services, information needed to deliver those services, such as business details, website content, and lead information captured on your behalf.
  • Automatically collected information — basic usage and device information when you visit our website, used to operate and improve the site.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services;
  • Manage and optimize our clients' online presence and listings;
  • Capture and deliver leads to our clients;
  • Communicate with you about your account, services, or inquiries;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Google User Data

For clients who authorize us to manage their Google Business Profile, we access certain Google data through Google APIs, with the client's explicit permission, solely to provide the services they have requested.

When a client grants us access to manage their Google Business Profile, we may access and use Google user data including business profile information, performance and insights data (such as how often the profile appears in search), and reviews. We use this data only to:

  • Display the client's own performance data back to them in their client dashboard;
  • Help manage and update their business listing;
  • Assist with managing and responding to reviews, with the client's direction.

Sentriq's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only access the data necessary to provide the services the client has requested;
  • We do not sell Google user data;
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising;
  • We do not transfer Google user data except as necessary to provide the service, comply with law, or as part of a business transfer, consistent with Google's requirements;
  • We do not allow humans to read Google user data unless we have the client's consent, it is necessary for security or to comply with law, or the data has been aggregated and anonymized.

SMS / Text Messaging

Some of our services involve sending and receiving SMS text messages on behalf of participating businesses — for example, an automated reply when a customer's phone call to a business is missed, followed by conversational messages responding to that customer's inquiry.

  • Consent: messages are sent only to individuals who first initiate contact (such as by calling a participating business's published phone number) or who otherwise consent to receive messages. We do not use purchased or marketing lists.
  • Message frequency varies based on the conversation. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-out: reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. You will receive one final confirmation message, after which no further messages will be sent. Reply HELP or email us for assistance.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties.
