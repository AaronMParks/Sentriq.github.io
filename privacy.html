Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 8, 2026

Sentriq Digital ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. It applies to our website (sentriqdigital.com), our services, and our SMS/text messaging programs.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide — such as your name, business name, email address, and phone number — when you contact us, request an audit or report, or become a client.
  • Message content — if you communicate with us or with a business we serve via SMS, we process the phone number and the content of those messages to provide the service.
  • Basic website analytics — standard technical data such as pages visited and general location, used to understand and improve our website.

How we use information

  • To provide and improve our services, including responding to inquiries and delivering requested reports;
  • To send and receive SMS messages in connection with our text messaging programs (described below);
  • To communicate about services you've requested or engaged us for;
  • To meet legal and compliance obligations.

SMS / text messaging privacy

Our text messaging programs send messages only to individuals who have first initiated contact (for example, by calling a participating business's phone number or by providing their number and consenting to receive texts). Message frequency varies based on the conversation. Message and data rates may apply.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing to subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other use case categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with, or sold to, any third parties.

You can opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After you send STOP, you will receive one final message confirming your opt-out, and no further messages will be sent. For help, reply HELP or contact us at hello@sentriqdigital.com.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (such as messaging and hosting providers), and only as needed to provide the service; with a business you have contacted, so it can respond to your inquiry; or when required by law.

Data retention & security

We retain information only as long as needed to provide services and meet legal obligations, and we use reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information appropriately.

Your choices

  • Opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP;
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by emailing hello@sentriqdigital.com;
  • Decline to provide information — though some services may not be available without it.

Children's privacy

Our services are intended for business use and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.
